Does graduating from this course help with job prospects?

Success in getting a job is supported by the abilities and competencies of each student. We encourage students to follow and complete the training program to the best of their ability. We will try to help students to get a job.

Does studying at Koursa get a certificate?

koursa provides digital certificates for students/participants who successfully complete the learning program.

Do you need to have an IT background to study at Koursa?

No, we will provide training for all prospective participants with whatever educational background they have.
